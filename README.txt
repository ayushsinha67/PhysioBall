PHYSIOBALL

--------------------------------------

This project has a medical application.The setup consists of a pressure sensor wrapped around a rubber ball. It measures compression. A counter increments with its current value displayed on the LCD whenever the pressure applied on the ball is beyond a user-defined threshold. Additionaly,  records of previous sessions are stored in the EEPROM of the AVR.

Componensts:

1. AVR ATmega 32
2. Pressure Sensor
3. 16 X 2 LCD
